Why did we make Cakeballs a Business

Hello this is the Founder of Chris's Creative Cakeballs, Chris Burnette.

My inspiration for our Company started with a question. I worked with an amazing woman who made Cakeballs for one particular Mother's Day. I tried one and to my surprise they tasted Amazing. I asked "how EASY was it to make the mini cakes?”. Little did I know, there was no easy way. The first batch I produced were okay but I wanted to make a Cakeball that tasted like or even better than the one I had for Mother’s Day. So, I was hooked on getting them right. I was fixed on making the perfect Cakeball.

After trial and error I began to learn and most importantly appreciate the process. From ‘adding the precise amount of batter into the mold’ to ‘melting chocolate for the right amount of time’, every step has been refined and tried so it could be the very best Cakeball there was. My wife and I fell in love with the process.

Now 5 years later and Thousands of Cakeballs and Cakepops made, we can proudly say “We have mastered the process”. We listened to customers feedback, how they experienced their order, if their expectations were met and many other questions. We get nothing but rave reviews.

Chris’s Creative Cakeball’s will speak for themselves. The smell and the look will have you hooked.
